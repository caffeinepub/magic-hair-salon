# Specification

## Summary
**Goal:** Build a single-page static website for "Magic Hair Salon" with a luxurious, premium aesthetic that showcases services, location, and contact information.

**Planned changes:**
- Hero section with salon name, open/closed status badge ("Open – Closes at 9:00 PM"), 4.9/5 star rating with 265 reviews, and a WhatsApp booking button linking to `wa.me/+918129529732`
- Services section with 5 category cards: Hair Services, Skin & Beauty, Body Services, Bridal & Event Services, and Other — each listing all specified services
- Location & Contact section with full address, clickable phone link (`tel:+918129529732`), WhatsApp chat button, and a "Get Directions" link to Google Maps
- Warm champagne, deep charcoal, and gold accent color palette with elegant typography, soft gradient backgrounds, and card-based layouts throughout
- Fully mobile-responsive design across all sections

**User-visible outcome:** Visitors can view the salon's services, check open status and rating, get directions, and initiate a WhatsApp booking — all from a single elegant, mobile-friendly page.
